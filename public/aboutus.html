Who We Are

Numeric Veda is more than just an educational platform; it's a journey towards unlocking your true potential. Led by Dr. Rakesh Bhatia, our esteemed classes on Zoom offer a unique blend of academic excellence and practical application. With a focus on mathematics, our programs cater to students, teachers, and government exam aspirants alike.

About Dr. Rakesh Bhatia

Dr. Rakesh Bhatia is a distinguished educator with a Ph.D. in Vedic Mathematics. With over 22 years of experience, he has been teaching mathematics at the National Institute Vidya Bhartiya Akhil Bhartiya Shiksha Sansthan in New Delhi. Dr. Bhatia serves as the National Co-coordinator of Vedic Mathematics in Shiksha Sanskriti Utthan Nyas, New Delhi, since 2019. He has conducted over 650 workshops on Vedic Mathematics in schools, colleges, and universities in India and abroad.

Dr. Bhatia is a prolific author and researcher, having published numerous papers and articles on Vedic Mathematics. He has also contributed to curriculum development and textbook writing for various educational institutions. Dr. Bhatia's passion for teaching extends to the college and university levels, where he has served as a resource person and guest lecturer at prestigious institutions across India. He has presented papers at numerous international and national conferences, solidifying his reputation as a leading authority in Vedic Mathematics.

With his wealth of knowledge and expertise, Dr. Rakesh Bhatia's classes offer a unique opportunity for students to learn from a true master of the subject. His dedication to education and tireless efforts to promote Vedic Mathematics make him an invaluable asset to Numeric Veda.
